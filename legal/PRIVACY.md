# Privacy Policy — Sudoku Daily

**Effective date:** 2026-05-30
**Last updated:** 2026-05-30

This Privacy Policy describes how Sudoku Daily ("we", "us", "our", the "App") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application or website.

---

## 1. Information we collect

### 1.1 Information you provide directly
- **Account info** — email address and password (only if you choose to create an account). Anonymous play is supported and does not require this.
- **Profile** — optional display name, avatar selection, country.
- **In-app communication** — messages you send to support.

### 1.2 Information collected automatically
- **Game data** — puzzle completion times, mistakes, hints used, scores, streak, level, XP, coins, inventory, equipped items.
- **Device info** — device type, OS version, app version, language, time zone.
- **Usage analytics** — feature usage, screens viewed, errors encountered. Sent via PostHog (privacy-friendly, no fingerprinting).
- **Crash reports** — stack traces and limited context via Sentry.

We do **not** collect: precise location, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, advertising IDs, or any biometric data.

---

## 2. How we use the information

| Purpose | Examples |
|---|---|
| Provide the service | Authenticate you, sync progress across devices, compute leaderboards, validate scores |
| Improve the app | Aggregate analytics to understand which features are used, fix crashes |
| Security & anti-cheat | Detect suspicious score submissions, prevent abuse |
| Communications | Respond to support requests; send important account / service notices |

We do **not** sell or rent your personal information.

---

## 3. Sharing of information

We share limited data only with:

- **Supabase** (backend host, EU/Asia regions) — stores accounts, game data, leaderboards.
- **PostHog** (analytics) — receives anonymized usage events.
- **Sentry** (crash reporting) — receives error reports.
- **Apple / Google** (when you install or update the app) — receives standard store-level diagnostics governed by their own privacy policies.

We may disclose information if required by law (e.g. valid subpoena) or to protect rights and safety.

---

## 4. Your choices

- **Play anonymously** — no email required. Anonymous accounts can be upgraded later without losing progress.
- **Delete your account** — sign in, go to Profile → Sign out → contact support@sudokudaily.app with your user ID. We delete account data within 30 days. Aggregated/anonymized data may be retained.
- **Opt out of analytics** — disable analytics in Settings (planned). For now, you can also block our analytics endpoint at the network level.
- **Export your data** — request via email; we'll send a JSON export within 30 days.

---

## 5. Children's privacy

Sudoku Daily is rated for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 provided us info, contact us and we will delete it.

---

## 6. Data retention

- Account data — kept while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of an account-deletion request.
- Anonymous accounts — auto-pruned after 12 months of inactivity.
- Leaderboard entries — kept for historical record (your row is anonymized on account deletion).
- Crash reports — purged after 90 days.

---

## 7. Security

We use industry-standard transport encryption (HTTPS) and at-rest encryption via Supabase. We use Row-Level Security to ensure users can only access their own data. Passwords are hashed by Supabase Auth (bcrypt). No system is perfectly secure — please use a strong unique password.

---

## 8. International transfers

We are based in Thailand. Backend services may operate in EU, US, and Asia-Pacific regions. By using the app, you consent to processing in those regions.

---

## 9. Changes to this policy

We will post any changes here and update the "Last updated" date. Material changes will be notified in-app or by email.
